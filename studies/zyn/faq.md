# ZYN Study FAQ

## What is the purpose of this study?

The purpose of this study is to evaluate the impact of ZYN nicotine pouch flavors on cigarette usage among adult smokers aged 22-65.

## How long does the study last?

The study lasts approximately 13.5 months, during which participants will complete regular check-ins and assessments.

## What is the compensation?

Total compensation for completing the study is $2,590. You will receive $20 for the first virtual screening and $25 for the second virtual screening. Additional payments are made at each study milestone.

## What are the visit requirements?

Participants are required to complete a remote visit every two weeks using our eResearch application. You will need a smartphone with text message and data capabilities.

## Where are the study sites located?

Rose Research Center has study sites in Raleigh and Charlotte, North Carolina. You will be assigned to the site closest to your location.

## What products are involved in the study?

The study involves ZYN nicotine pouches. Participants who currently smoke combustible cigarettes will be provided with ZYN products as part of the research protocol.
